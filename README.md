**Key metrics tracked:** Total Sales ($899.9M), Units Sold (2M), Total Profit ($332.1M), Average Operating Margin (42.30%)

## Key Insights
- Men's Footwear is the highest revenue driver, this simply means that Adidas's core strength in terms of products lies on it.


- Certain Regions such as Northeast performed better than others hence the need for regional concentration.


- There is strong dominance  by In-store sales method and this may cause digital channel such as tools ,technologies not to be  used  effectively.

- There's also strong dominance by retailers for instance, West Gear Footlocker contribute majority of revenue meaning that sales depends heavily on few retailers.


-There's healthy  profitability and strong pricing strategy hence Average Operating margin on 42%

## Recommendations
- Since Men's footwear is the highest generating  driver, Adidas should continue investing in product innovation, inventory expansion within the segment to maintain market leadership.


- High performing regions such as Northeast should  be used as a benchmark for other regions. Successful sales strategy, customer engagement across underperforming regions to improve regional performance should be replicated.


- The dominance of Instore Sales suggests that digital channels are underutilized and therefore should be introduced, Boost Online sales, e-commerce platforms, mobile shopping experience etc.

- Adidas should diversify  partnership to reduce dependency risks and improve market stability.

- With average  operating margin on 42%, Adidas demonstrate strong profitability and effective market strategies. the company should maintain this pricing efficiency while exploring opportunities for cost optimization and product positioning.
